Great Inventions

The breakthroughs that changed civilisation — from the wheel to the microchip.

The Power of Human Ingenuity

Throughout history, a handful of inventions have fundamentally altered the course of human civilisation. Each one solved a pressing problem, then went on to unlock possibilities no one had imagined.

What follows is a timeline of some of the most transformative inventions ever created.

The Ripple Effect

Great inventions rarely stand alone. The printing press enabled the Scientific Revolution. The steam engine powered the Industrial Revolution. The transistor gave birth to the Information Age.

Each breakthrough builds on what came before, creating a compounding effect of progress.

"If I have seen further, it is by standing on the shoulders of giants." — Isaac Newton
